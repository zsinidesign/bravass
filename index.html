About

These workshops are not lectures. They are interactive thinking labs where participants leave with concrete insights, directions and ideas.

From Idea to Concept

This workshop helps you move from "I have an idea" to "This is my next project and how it starts." We clarify the idea, define the problem worth solving, and use simple design-thinking tools to shape it into a clear concept and a simple prototype you can test.

What participants do

  • Identify the problem worth solving
  • Define the core concept
  • Create a simple prototype worth testing

Outcome

  • A clear idea concept
  • A simple, testable prototype
  • A concrete next step for development
  • Confidence to move from thinking to doing

Perfect for

Creatives, solopreneurs, and professionals exploring new projects

Inner Leadership

True leadership starts from within. This workshop helps you develop self-awareness, emotional resilience, and the inner clarity needed to lead with authenticity and purpose — especially in uncertain times.

What participants do

  • Explore personal leadership and behaviour patterns
  • Identify core values and strengths
  • Practice self-reflection and emotional regulation

Outcome

  • Greater self-awareness and emotional clarity
  • A personal leadership compass to guide your decisions and boundaries
  • A 3-step framework to act with purpose, even when things feel unclear
  • More confidence to lead yourself before leading others

Perfect for

Professionals who want to lead with authenticity and less burnout

Wellbeing in the Chaos

In times of uncertainty it's easy to feel overwhelmed and mentally scattered. This workshop helps you regain clarity, emotional balance, and a clearer sense of direction so you can move forward with intention instead of reaction.

What participants do

  • Map your current mental and emotional landscape
  • Reconnect with your personal strengths and internal resources
  • Design small, realistic steps to move forward in your life or work

Outcome

  • Greater mental clarity and reduced feeling of overwhelm
  • A renewed connection to your personal strengths and how to use them
  • Practical tools and mini-rituals to navigate uncertainty
  • A short, doable action plan for the next 2–4 weeks

Perfect for

Navigating change, transitions, or complex life phases
