About

I facilitate thinking spaces where people slow down, ask better questions, and design their next steps with clarity.

My background combines:

  • Research
  • Human-centered strategy

These workshops are not lectures. They are interactive thinking labs where participants leave with concrete insights, directions and ideas.

From Idea to Concept

This workshop helps you move from "I have an idea" to "This is my next project and how it starts." Using design-thinking exercises, you clarify the real problem, define a clear concept and your concrete next steps.

What you get

  • One clearly defined project concept
  • A defined problem statement and target audience
  • A simple prototype or first step to test within two weeks
  • Concrete next steps so you stop staying stuck in your head

Designed for

Freelancers, creatives and early-stage founders

Inner Leadership

True leadership starts from within. This workshop helps you develop self-awareness, emotional resilience and the inner clarity needed to lead with authenticity and purpose.

  • Identify core values and strengths
  • Practice self-reflection and emotional regulation
  • Design a personal leadership compass

Outcome

  • Deeper understanding of your leadership style
  • Greater self-awareness and emotional clarity
  • A personal leadership compass to guide decisions
  • Tools for leading with authenticity
